## 🏗️ Notable Projects

### 🥽 KovilLens — Mixed Reality for Cultural Heritage
*Apr 2023 – Feb 2024 | Major Project — Published at ICVR 2025*

Integrated a detailed 3D reconstruction of the **Moovar Kovil temple** into the **HoloLens mixed-reality environment**, with stable placement, proper scaling, and accurate spatial alignment. Validated for historical accuracy and contextual sensitivity by academic experts. Published as *"KovilLens: A Mixed Reality Application for Digital Preservation and Exploration of Moovar Kovil"* at the **11th International Conference on Virtual Reality (ICVR 2025)**, Wageningen, Netherlands.

**Tech:** Unity · Microsoft HoloLens · Mixed Reality Toolkit (MRTK)
